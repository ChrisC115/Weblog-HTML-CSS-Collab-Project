Quantum Entanglement

How it Works.

Linked States:
Entangled particles behave as a single system. They lose their individual identities and their properties become correlated.
Instantaneous correlation:
This correlation holds true no matter how far apart the particles are. If you measure a property of one particle, the state of the other is instantly determined.
Theories of Entanglement

It has recently been suggested that a similar process occurs between people and explains anomalous phenomena such as healing."
